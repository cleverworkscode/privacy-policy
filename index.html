Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 7, 2026

Formula Run ("the App") is developed by Cleverworks Code. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

When you use Formula Run, we collect the following information:

  • Account information — When you sign in with Apple, we receive your Apple ID identifier and, if you choose to share it, your name. We store a display name you provide during profile setup.
  • Running data — Times you submit for race events within your league, including timestamps and any applicable penalties.
  • League data — League names, team names, and membership information necessary to operate your private league.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to provide the App's functionality:

  • Authenticating your identity
  • Displaying your profile and running times to members of your league
  • Computing standings and rankings within your league

We do not use your data for advertising, analytics, or any purpose beyond the App's core features.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Cloud Firestore and Firebase Authentication), hosted on Google Cloud infrastructure. Data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or disclose your personal information to third parties. Your running times and profile are only visible to other members of leagues you join.

Tracking

Formula Run does not track you across other apps or websites. We do not use advertising identifiers or third-party analytics services.

Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained as long as your account exists. You can delete your account at any time from the Profile screen within the App. When you delete your account:

  • Your user profile is permanently deleted
  • Your submitted times and league memberships are removed
  • If you are a league administrator, the league and all its data are also deleted

Children's Privacy

Formula Run is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at: privacy@cleverworks.com
